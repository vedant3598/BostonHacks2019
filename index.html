About Us

About G-Nome

G-Nome is an innovative, extraordinary solution to solving a major issue that plagues patients who are tremendously ill. We provide services such as personalized care-taking such as making calls and booking doctor appointments, tracking daily mood and symptoms in a individualized log book. It helps patients from being lonely and aids to lighten the mood of patient, plays inspirational music, and provides exercises for improving their mood.

Vision

Provide personalized, easy to install, smart-home devices to improve the quality of life for patients worldwide

F.A.Q

  • What does G-Nome do?

    G-nome provides a personalized experience tailored for the patient including medication reminders, symptom tracking and appointment making through a voice-activated care system.

  • What does a nurse do?

    Nurses are the most trusted healthcare professionals. They are with patients throughout the continuum of life and provide both emotional and physical care. G-nome does not replace the importance of trained registered nurses and doctors.

  • Why is G-nome important in our changing medical landscape?

    There is a nursing shortage in North America. Due to an influx of patients (the aging population) into the health system, the retirement of baby boomers, and educational bottlenecks, nursing positions aren't being filled fast enough to keep up with demand.

  • How we came up with this?

    We put ourselves in the shoes of disease sufferers, most specifically Lou Gehrig's disease for this project. We empathize with sufferers of ALS and conceptualized tools that could make their lives a little easier. Some common complications with ALS include muscle spasms, cramps and fatigue. Additionally, some medications have to be taken consistently everyday for successful treatment. To help ease the burden of these issues, we have implemented a tracker for symptoms and medication reminders through Alexa.
